President

Ashok Acharya

President, Nepalese Community in Busan (NCB)

Namaste and warm greetings to all! On behalf of the Nepalese Community in Busan (NCB), I extend my heartfelt welcome to every Nepali brother, sister, and friend living in this beautiful city of Busan and across South Korea. Our community was established with a shared vision — to bring together Nepalese living in Busan under one roof of unity, mutual support, and cultural pride.

Over the years, NCB has grown into a vibrant family where we celebrate our festivals, preserve our traditions, and support one another in times of need. Whether it is organizing cultural events, assisting newcomers, or collaborating with local institutions, our goal remains the same: to promote solidarity, cultural understanding, and the positive image of Nepal in Korea.

Busan is not only a city of opportunities but also a place that embraces diversity. Through our collective effort, we hope to strengthen the bridge between Nepal and Korea, showcasing the spirit, resilience, and warmth of the Nepalese people.

Let's continue to move forward with unity, respect, and pride in who we are.
